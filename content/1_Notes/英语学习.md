- **abandon**
    
    释义：give up (stop trying/keeping)
    
    短句：She had to abandon her old bike.
    
- **ability**
    
    释义：the ability to do something well
    
    短句：His ability to sing surprised everyone.
    
- **able**
    
    释义：can (be able to do)
    
    短句：I am able to finish the work today.
    
- **abortion**
    
    释义：ending a pregnancy before birth
    
    短句：The doctor explained the abortion process.
